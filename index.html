The Ethos

Rooted in Inglewood. Made for the Night.

Harmony by Lavender Blue is a Black woman-owned restaurant and lounge built on a simple belief: great food, great music, and great people belong in the same room.

From live R&B and jazz to elevated California cuisine, handcrafted cocktails, private celebrations, and unforgettable game days, Harmony was created to bring people together with style, warmth, and soul.

Inspired by the culture and creative energy of Inglewood, Harmony is a place to meet after work, celebrate with friends, discover a new favorite artist, catch the game, or settle into a night that feels worth remembering.
